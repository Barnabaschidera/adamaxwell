Who We Are

Built for the Energy Frontline

Ada Maxwell Oil & Gas Nigeria Limited is a dynamic, multi-faceted organization at the forefront of the energy, logistics, and general merchandise industries. With a legacy of excellence and innovation, we specialize in providing world-class services in oil and gas, clearing and forwarding, and general merchandise to clients across the globe. Our strategic approach to business ensures that we offer tailored solutions that drive efficiency, safety, and sustainability in every sector we serve.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between industries, AMOG Group is driven by a commitment to operational excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement. We leverage decades of experience and deep industry knowledge to deliver unmatched service quality, both locally and internationally.

Our team combines deep industry knowledge with hands-on field experience, enabling us to provide reliable, cost-effective services that keep your operations running safely and efficiently.
